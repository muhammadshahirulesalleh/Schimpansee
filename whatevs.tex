Objective of the game:
Flip card – match card
How the game works:
-at start of the game, the cards are shuffled and placed face down
-if the cards get a correct match, the player gets another turn
-if the cards get a wrong match, the opponent gets the turn
-the winner is decided by who has the highest score
Ethic concern and limitation:
-to be able to train the chimps to even be able to play this, it requires months of works and constant feedback looping mechanisms, it is also noted that, this project can end in a failure if the chimps simply do not care about the game and does not engage with it, it is through the feedback looping mechanism where the chimps might want to play and engage with this game. Simply said there are no guarantees that the chimps will play this game
-just like humans, the chimps are also a living being, capable of thinking, having an emotion and also feel suffering. It is to be noted that the chimps are not deprived of food or any other necessity while doing this project. This project only serves as an enrichment towards the monkey, so that they are able to have fun and engage with the visitors and also be rewarded with extra food/treats for their excellent play against the human
